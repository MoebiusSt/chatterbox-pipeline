Community Singing  [lang="de"]ist ein Bereich der Community Music. Was gemeinschaftliches Singen in diesem Zusammenhang bedeutet und welche Rolle Leitung in singenden Gruppen spielt, erläutert Marion Haak-Schulenburg im Gespräch mit dem folker.[/lang]

The same applied for his compatriot  [lang="fr"]Julien Bruneteau[/lang] who, having studied jazz flute, developed his distinctive crossover style.

Christensen & Kanne Folkband are probably the longest-serving Danish group with their Album [lang="da"]Vi Drømmer Stadig[/lang] shooting to the top one hundret.
